Rules / How to play

Can you recognise lyrics? Or is 15s of a song enough for you to identify it? Think you are an expert in a certain artist or genre?

Test your music knowledge now!

Artist mode:
Play from songs by the selected artist

Genre mode:
Be tested on songs from the selected genre

Lyric/clips:
Have only lyrics or clips as clues, or chicken out and get both

Timer mode:
You only get 15s to guess

Show Artist (in Genre and Mix mode):
Game will diplay which artist the song belongs to

Scoring Mode:
Basic: Play 10 rounds!
Staying Alive:(requires Timer Mode: On) Start with 10000 points
You lose points as time passes (up to 1000pts in 15s).
You lose 1000pts if you run out of time
You lose 500pts if you guess wrongly
Guess as many as you can before you run out of points!
